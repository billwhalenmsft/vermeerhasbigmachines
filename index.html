Microsoft · Dynamics 365 Contact Center

Thank you, Vermeer — here's your agentic contact center, on one platform

A short recap of what we showed and how Dynamics 365 answers each part of your contact-center evaluation — voice, AI, CRM, routing, and reporting, all on a single Microsoft platform.

Why one platform

Simplicity + AI = acceleration

Most contact-center modernizations stitch together a calling platform, a separate CRM, and a carrier. Microsoft brings them together — so your teams work in one place, your data stays consistent, and AI can act across the whole journey.

◆

One platform

Voice, chat, email, cases, knowledge, AI and analytics are the same Dynamics 365 — one data model, not a CRM bolted to a calling system.

✦

AI that acts

A real-time voice agent answers, understands natural speech, looks up an order, and hands off to a representative with full context — no menus, no repeating.

⬡

Simpler operations

Skill-based routing replaces queue sprawl, and enterprise voice runs on Teams Phone — fewer moving parts to train, staff, and report on.

What we demonstrated

Mapped to your evaluation

Core agent experienceTelephony · Phase 1
Inbound calls route to the right team, the agent gets an automatic screen pop with the customer and case, and handles hold, mute, and warm/cold transfers from one desktop. Learn more ↗
Routing & scaleTelephony · Phase 2
Unified, skill-based routing assigns by priority, skill, and capacity — with overflow handling and customer callback. Learn more ↗
AI voice agentTelephony · Phase 3
A real-time voice agent takes natural speech and keypad input in the same call, identifies intent, performs an order-status lookup, and escalates to a representative with context. Learn more ↗
Performance visibilityTelephony · Phase 4
Real-time supervisor dashboards (agent state, queue, service level, wait time) plus historical analytics with export. Learn more ↗
Cases, knowledge & CopilotCRM · Phases 1–3
Cases are created from any channel, agents work them with knowledge and SLAs, and Copilot summarizes, suggests responses, and detects sentiment — grounded in your data. Learn more ↗
Security & governanceCRM · Phase 4
Role-based access, full audit, data retention, and AI that honors your security boundary — your data stays in your tenant. Learn more ↗
